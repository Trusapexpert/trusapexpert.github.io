Understanding the SAP FICO Module

SAP FICO (Financial Accounting and Controlling) is one of the most widely implemented core modules. Financial Accounting (FI) focuses on external reporting (General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable), while Controlling (CO) handles internal planning and monitoring (Cost Center, Profit Center Accounting).

Choosing the right training involves considering whether the curriculum is based on the older ECC platform or the newer, integrated **S/4HANA** system, which offers streamlined processes and the Universal Journal.
